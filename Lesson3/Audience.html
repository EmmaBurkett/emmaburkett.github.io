Target Audience

Middle class citizens with children in the age range of 10-17 years old. Also youth of the area, or adventurous adults.
Because the website is geared more towards vacations or tourists traps, it should look to attract people with money to spend hence, middle class citizens. The website will also feature cool/fun/new things to do, so it should also aim to attract the local youth population.
