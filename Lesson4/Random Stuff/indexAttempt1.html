Weather Summary

The purpose of this website, Blue Skies Forecasting, is to tell the public what days would be good for going outside. It is meant to attract tourists and to help the locals plan their activities. The website will display to the user the best upcoming days for outside activity.
It will not display bad weather days.
The website will also promote fun activities, and places to sight-see according to the day: if it’s going to be hot, the website will promote an ice cream shop versus if it’s cold the website will promote a hot chocolate shop.
In addition on another page the website will display upcoming activites in the area and the correlating weather, even if the weather is poor.

Five Day Forecast

The definition of a good weather day will be as follows:

  • Winter: Days with less snow or a warmer temperature above 33 degrees Fahrenheit. It will also list days with heavy snowfall because local snowball fights are important too!
  • Spring and Fall: Days without rain or days without icy roadways. Any warm day (warm being unusually warm for the region and the season).
  • Summer: Days above 70 degrees Fahrenheit.
